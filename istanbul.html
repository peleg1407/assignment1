Welcome to Istanbul

Istanbul, straddling the Bosphorus strait between Europe and Asia, is a city that has been at the crossroads of civilizations for centuries. Known for its historical mosques and vibrant bazaars, Istanbul offers a unique blend of the old and the new, providing a cultural enrichment unlike any other city.

Top Attractions in Istanbul

  • Hagia Sophia - A former Greek Orthodox Christian patriarchal basilica, later an Ottoman imperial mosque and now a museum, famous for its massive dome.
  • Topkapi Palace - The primary residence of the Ottoman Sultans for approximately 400 years, showcasing a rich collection of artifacts.
  • Blue Mosque - Known for its blue tiles surrounding the interior walls, it remains an active mosque while also being a popular tourist site.
  • Grand Bazaar - One of the largest and oldest covered markets in the world, with over 4,000 shops selling everything from spices to jewelry and ceramics.
  • Bosphorus Cruise - Offers a scenic view of the Istanbul skyline and the perfect way to see the blend of European and Asian influences.
