Welcome to Athens

Athens is a historic city known as the cradle of Western civilization and the birthplace of democracy. Home to ancient monuments and works of art, the most famous of all being the Parthenon, Athens encapsulates a blend of classical and modern features.

Top Attractions in Athens

  • Acropolis and the Parthenon - Iconic ancient archaeological wonders of the world.
  • National Archaeological Museum - Houses some of the most important artifacts from a variety of archaeological locations around Greece from prehistory to late antiquity.
  • Plaka - A historic neighborhood of Athens, clustered around the northern and eastern slopes of the Acropolis.
  • Ancient Agora - The heart of public life in ancient Athens, with many of the ruins remarkably preserved.
  • Syntagma Square - The central square of Athens, full of activity and historical significance.
