Welcome to Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and the principal commercial and financial center of the Netherlands. Known for its historical attractions, great art collections, and the distinctive color and flavor of its well-preserved old sections, it draws scores of tourists each year.

Top Attractions in Amsterdam

  • Rijksmuseum - The largest and most prestigious museum for art and history in the Netherlands.
  • Van Gogh Museum - Houses the largest collection of paintings by Vincent van Gogh.
  • The Anne Frank House - The hiding place where Anne Frank wrote her famous diary during World War II.
  • The Jordaan - A district known for its narrow streets, quaint buildings, and atmospheric canals.
  • Vondelpark - The largest city park in Amsterdam, popular with residents and tourists alike.
