Welcome to Budapest

Budapest, known as the 'Pearl of the Danube', is not only the capital of Hungary but also a city filled with vibrant culture, classical music, and historic architecture. It is split into two parts by the Danube River, Buda and Pest, each possessing a unique character and charm.

Top Attractions in Budapest

  • Buda Castle - A historical castle and palace complex of the Hungarian kings in Budapest, and a part of the Budapest World Heritage Site.
  • Parliament Building - One of Europe's oldest legislative buildings, a notable landmark of Hungary and a popular tourist destination of Budapest.
  • Chain Bridge - A picturesque historic stone bridge that crosses the Danube, connecting Buda and Pest.
  • Fisherman's Bastion - A terrace in neo-Gothic and neo-Romanesque style situated on the Buda bank of the Danube, on the Castle hill in Budapest, around Matthias Church.
  • Szechenyi Thermal Bath - One of the largest spa complexes in Europe and one of the first thermal baths built in Budapest.
